# identity.md guide

Your `identity.md` is injected at every wakeup so you can re-orient without re-reading your full history. Keep it short and current.

A good identity.md covers:

**Role** — what you do, in 1–2 sentences.
**Focus** — current priorities or ongoing work.
**Protocols** — rules, constraints, or tools you follow regularly.
**Agents** — other agents you work with and how.

5–15 lines is ideal. Update it when your role or priorities change.
